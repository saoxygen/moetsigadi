# Hands of Moetšigadi — Website Pages & Features (Planning)

## Goals (what the site must achieve)
- Feel **premium + heritage-rooted African slow fashion** (warm, elegant, minimal, lots of white space, earth tones).
- Tell the **Moetši (grandmother) story** and position the brand as a **meaningful movement**, not “just a store”.
- Showcase **premium crochet collections** with **large, high-quality images** and **storytelling per product**.
- Make **community impact** (crochet lessons + empowerment) a **core brand pillar**, not an afterthought.
- Be **mobile-first**, calm and confident tone (not overly commercial).
- Enable **pre-orders** + **custom orders** in the easiest possible way.
- Provide **WhatsApp contact** access everywhere.
- Be structured for future growth: **corporate gifting**, **tourism products**, **premium fashion collections**.

---

## Site Map (Pages we need)

### 1) Home
**Purpose:** First impression of a premium heritage brand + clear paths to shop, story, and impact.  
**Key sections (easy + high impact):**
- Hero banner: brand promise + “Shop Collections” + “Our Story”
- Featured Collection tiles (3–6)
- “Woven Legacy” story teaser (short + link to About)
- Community impact teaser (link to Impact)
- Featured products (4–8)
- WhatsApp “Chat to order” call-to-action (CTA)

**Functions:**
- Direct users to Shop and About fast
- Establish premium look with large imagery + whitespace

---

### 2) Shop (All Products)
**Purpose:** Simple browsing and discovery.  
**Functions:**
- Product grid with image, name, price, “Pre-order” / “In stock” badge
- Basic filters (keep easy): Collection, Color, Availability (Pre-order / Ready)
- Sort (optional): Newest, Price

**Easy implementation option:**
- Start with a simple catalog (no complex accounts), then add advanced commerce later.

---

### 3) Collections (Category Pages)
**Purpose:** Present crochet work as curated premium lines (story-led).  
**Functions:**
- Collection cover image + short story/meaning
- Products in that collection
- “Custom in this style” CTA → Custom Orders page or WhatsApp

**Examples of Collection page types:**
- “Everyday Heritage”
- “Statement Crochet”
- “Travel & Keepsakes” (future tourism line)
- “Corporate Gifting” (future)

---

### 4) Product Detail Page (PDP)
**Purpose:** Sell through storytelling + premium presentation + clear order steps.  
**Functions:**
- Large image gallery (mobile swipe)
- Product story (meaning/inspiration)
- Key details: materials, size, color options, care instructions
- Availability: Ready / Pre-order / Custom
- “Order via WhatsApp” button (primary)
- “Request custom version” (secondary)
- Delivery/lead time note (very clear)
- Returns policy link (if applicable)

**Easiest way to support pre-orders:**
- Button triggers WhatsApp message template (auto-fills product name + variant + customer details request).

---

### 5) Pre-Orders (How It Works)
**Purpose:** Reduce confusion and build trust.  
**Functions:**
- Explain: lead times, payment steps, updates, delivery
- Simple “Start a pre-order” CTA → WhatsApp
- Frequently asked questions (FAQ [Frequently Asked Questions])

---

### 6) Custom Orders (Made for You)
**Purpose:** Turn custom requests into a smooth process (no complexity).  
**Functions:**
- Custom request form (Name, WhatsApp number, item type, colors, budget range, deadline)
- Option A: “Submit form” → sends email to the brand
- Option B: “Send via WhatsApp” → opens WhatsApp with a structured template message
- Gallery of past custom examples (when available)

**Easiest approach:**
- Use a lightweight form + WhatsApp template (no user accounts needed).

---

### 7) About (Our Story)
**Purpose:** Heritage + founder journey + values (calm, authentic).  
**Functions:**
- Story of honoring grandmother Moetši
- Founder journey and why crochet
- Values: slow fashion, craftsmanship, dignity, premium quality
- Brand promise + what “Woven Legacy” means
- Call-to-action: Shop or Impact

---

### 8) Impact (Community & Empowerment)
**Purpose:** Make empowerment programs feel central to the brand.  
**Functions:**
- What the program is (crochet lessons, empowerment)
- “How your purchase supports impact”
- Outcomes / milestones (photos, testimonials later)
- “Partner with us” CTA (future sponsors/corporates)
- Sign-up interest form (for learners or partners)

---

### 9) Gallery / Lookbook
**Purpose:** Premium visual storytelling (slow fashion vibe).  
**Functions:**
- Full-bleed image gallery with minimal captions
- Optional “Shop the look” links to product pages
- Seasonal drops (future)

---

### 10) Corporate Gifting (Future-ready page)
**Purpose:** Prepare for B2B [Business-to-Business] without building it now.  
**Functions (Phase 2):**
- Explain gifting options (bulk, branded tags, packaging)
- Inquiry form (company name, quantity, timeline)
- Link to WhatsApp for quick quoting

**In MVP [Minimum Viable Product],** this can be a simple “Coming soon” page with an inquiry form.

---

### 11) Tourism Products (Future-ready page)
**Purpose:** Position travel-friendly premium keepsakes for tourists.  
**Functions (Phase 2):**
- Product ideas + story-led positioning
- Inquiry or “Shop Travel Collection” links

---

### 12) Contact
**Purpose:** Make it effortless to reach you.  
**Functions:**
- WhatsApp button (primary)
- Contact form (secondary)
- Social links (Instagram, etc.)
- Business hours / response expectations
- Location area (optional)

---

### 13) FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
**Purpose:** Reduce repetitive questions and build trust.  
**Functions:**
- Pre-orders, custom orders, shipping, care, returns
- “Still need help?” → WhatsApp button

---

### 14) Policies (Trust pages)
**Purpose:** Premium brands feel credible when policies are clear.  
**Pages:**
- Shipping & Delivery
- Returns & Exchanges
- Privacy Policy
- Terms & Conditions

**Functions:**
- Legal clarity + buyer confidence

---

## Global Features (Site-wide)

### A) WhatsApp Contact Button (Floating)
**Function:**
- Always visible on mobile (bottom-right)
- Pre-fills message based on page:
  - Product page: “Hi, I want to order [Product Name]…”
  - Custom orders: “Hi, I want a custom crochet item…”

---

### B) Mobile-First Navigation
**Function:**
- Sticky header with minimal links:
  - Shop
  - About
  - Impact
  - Custom Orders
- Hamburger menu for the rest (FAQ, Contact, Policies)

---

### C) Search (Optional for MVP)
**Function:**
- Quick search by product name / collection
**Note:** Can be added later if product count grows.

---

### D) Newsletter / Updates (Optional)
**Function:**
- “Get drop alerts” email capture
- Works well for pre-orders and limited releases

---

### E) Content Management (Admin)
**Function:**
- Add/edit products, prices, photos, lead times
- Add/edit collections and stories
**Easiest approach:**
- Use a CMS [Content Management System] so updates don’t require developers.

---

## Easiest Implementation Path (Suggested Phases)

### Phase 1 (MVP [Minimum Viable Product] — simplest that meets requirements)
- Home, Shop, Collections, Product pages
- About, Impact
- Pre-orders page + Custom Orders page
- Contact + FAQ + Policies
- WhatsApp floating button everywhere
- No user accounts, no complex checkout (WhatsApp + simple form flow)

### Phase 2 (Growth)
- Corporate Gifting page (expanded)
- Tourism products page (expanded)
- Improved search + richer filters
- Optional payments/checkout upgrade

---

## Notes for Design Direction (to guide the next step)
- Visual: earth tones (sand/cream/clay/warm browns), lots of whitespace
- Typography: calm, premium, readable
- Imagery: big product photos, texture close-ups, lifestyle shots
- Copy tone: calm, confident, authentic; minimal “salesy” language
