Our Story

Woven in Memory, Made with Purpose

Hands of Moetšigadi began as a tribute to my grandmother Moetši, whose crochet work taught patience, craftsmanship, and the beauty of creating with intention.

What started as a personal journey has grown into a premium slow-fashion brand rooted in heritage, dignity, and handmade excellence.
