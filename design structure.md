# Hands of Moetšigadi — Home Page Design

## Design Principles

The homepage must feel like entering a **heritage craft house**, not an ecommerce store.

Key design traits:

- Calm
- Premium
- Handmade
- Heritage-driven
- Slow fashion storytelling

Design rules:

- Large imagery
- Lots of whitespace
- Soft earth tones
- Minimal text
- Mobile-first layout


----------------------------------------------------
